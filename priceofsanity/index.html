The Price of Sanity

The Price of Sanity is a tower defense game with a focus on casting spells. It was my second school game project, made in a team of 16 people. I served as one of 2 QA for the project.

Play on Itch.io

QA & Technical Verification

My role involved deep testing of complex interactions, VFX verification, and maintaining clear communication with the engineering and art teams to resolve technical debt. I reported 22 bugs and some other issues. Beyond functional testing, I performed spelling and grammar checks for all in-game text and documentation.
