Chenso Club

Chenso Club is a roguelike 2D platformer featuring a squad of heroines slaughtering whacky alien characters. It's a delightful, playfully gory game, with a lot of heart. This was one of the first games our class was assigned to play as an introduction to QA testing.

Check it out on Steam

QA Methodology & Teamwork

Working with a prototype build, our class performed structured testing, peer reviews, and bug reporting, divided into groups of 3. We developed comprehensive documentation including test plans and test case sheets. This project was a pivotal learning experience in the QA workflow - teamwork, work division, and professional documentation.
