Emoji Detective: Hidden Object

Emoji Detective: Hidden Object is a casual roguelike hidden objects game made for iOS and Android. This project was my introduction to professional Mobile QA, where I learned to navigate Android emulation, perform deep debugging, and communicate findings to a development team for a live-service casual game.

QA & Mobile Testing

Working as external testers during our Mobile testing module, our class of 12 QA students performed deep testing on a prototype build. I contributed 9 detailed bug reports and 5 strategic feature requests. My testing focused on UI/UX responsiveness and monetization loops, specifically verifying ad-reward systems and state persistence between rounds.
