Growbot: Toy to Titan
Growbot is an arcade style city destruction game, made primarily for a younger audience. It was my first school game project, made with a team of 15 people.

I did QA work for the game, including testing, providing feedback, reporting bugs, recording feedback from playtesters, as well as documentation such as a smoke sheet. I wrote a total of 19 bug reports for the game.

I also acted as Product Owner for the project. I helped create and held presentations, assisted with structuring, participated in design, and assisted in Jira management; setting up the board, routines, as well as backlog maintenance.
