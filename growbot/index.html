Growbot: Toy to Titan
Growbot is an arcade style city destruction game, made primarily for a younger audience. It was my first school game project.

I did QA work for the game, including testing, providing feedback, reporting bugs, recording feedback from testers, as well as documentation such as a smoke sheet. A total of 19 bug reports were made.

I also acted as Product Owner for the project. I helped create and held presentations, assisted with structuring, participated in design, and assisted Jira, such as setting up board, routines, as well as backlog maintenance.
